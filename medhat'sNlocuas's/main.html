Applied Technology Schools


Technical education is: a type of formal education carried out by formal educational institutions
for a period of three or five years after completing the preparatory stage,
or two years after the completion of the secondary stage,
which enables the student to acquire the competencies necessary to prepare him to work in a certain profession,
and includes all schools and technical and vocational centers. Of its types and stages,
affiliated with the Ministry of Education and Technical Education

Dual education schools, industrial apprenticeship schools and centers affiliated
with the Productive Efficiency Department of the Ministry of Trade and Industry,
secondary nursing schools affiliated with the Ministry of Health and Population,
upper-intermediate technical institutes affiliated with the Ministry of Higher Education and Scientific Research,
and technical schools affiliated with the private sector and civil society,
whose curricula are approved by the Ministry of Education and Technical Education.
